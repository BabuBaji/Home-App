Privacy Policy

HomeHelp respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

We collect your mobile number for login/OTP verification, and—if you sign in with Google—your name, email, and profile image where available.

Your information is used to create and secure your account, process bookings, and improve the service. We never sell your data.

Mobile numbers are masked in logs, OTPs are never exposed, and Google tokens are validated securely on our backend.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time from your profile settings.
